About Us - Thaalam Media

Welcome to Thaalam Media, a trailblazing organization with an illustrious 20-year legacy in media-related activities. Since our inception in 2002 in Sri Lanka, we have forged an extensive network in the media landscape, becoming more than just a broadcasting entity. Thaalam Media stands proud as a successful organizer of mega events, featuring top movie and TV personalities, with over 190 events executed seamlessly.

Thaalam in Switzerland

In the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland, Thaalam has made significant strides, establishing itself as a pioneer in Tamil media. Our radio, Thaalam FM, is broadcast across Switzerland using advanced Digital Audio Broadcasting (DAB) technology, marking a historic milestone as the first and only Tamil radio station in the country. Thaalam is the official radio station covering the entire Swiss territory, spanning all 26 Cantons.

Thaalam Swiss has sparked a music revolution, catering to the Tamil diaspora's longing for a radio station in their language for the past 40 years. Broadcasting in high-definition DAB, Thaalam reaches listeners in their vehicles and homes, captivating 80% of the youth demographic despite the plethora of entertainment options available.

Thaalam's Commitment to Music and Youth

Thaalam places paramount importance on music, boasting a dedicated department attuned to the pulse of our listeners. Our commitment is reflected in our stronghold on 80% of the youth audience who actively engage with Thaalam's programs, distinguishing us as a leader among radio stations.

Our divisions, Thaalam Creative and Thaalam News, aim to position Thaalam as a global news network. We take pride in bucking the trend, championing the inclusion of the current generation Tamil diaspora, bridging the gap between their mother tongue and the language of their residence.

Expertise in Mega Events

As a mega media organization, Thaalam possesses the expertise, network, team, and resources to orchestrate mega events successfully. We are the only media organization equipped to conduct these events seamlessly, solidifying our reputation as a credible and trustworthy entity.

AR Rahman Event in Zurich - October 2023

In October 2023, Thaalam Media orchestrated a historic event at Zurich's Hallenstadion featuring the musical maestro AR Rahman. This back-to-back two-day extravaganza marked a milestone in Rahman's 30-year musical journey, attracting an unprecedented 18,750 enthusiastic music lovers. The event included an unforgettable after-party attended by more than 3,000 people, making it the unmatched show of Rahman's recent Europe Tour.

Paranthu Pogiren with Pradeep Kumar Event in Colombo - March 2023

In March 2023, Thaalam Media presented the mega event "Paranthu Pogiren with Pradeep Kumar" at Colombo's Sugathadasa Indoor Stadium. This mammoth success, held as a back-to-back houseful show, surpassed all previous events in the Tamil Film Industry over the last 20 years. With unparalleled success, it stood as the superlative hit in Sri Lanka, marking the largest event in the country in the past four years.
